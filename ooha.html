Out Of Home Adoption: a way to co-exist with our fellow species

OOHA or Out Of Home Adoption is a campaign to inspire and urge people to help street animals (streetys) in and around their neighborhood.

Take care of the animal(s) on a regular basis as their own pets There are 5 steps to doing the OOHA

Why OOHA

Indi streetys go unattended and starve most of the time. 

We may love them all, but do not or cannot bring them home

No medical facilities reach them. They die ill and way before their time.

It's got tougher for them to survive during the pandemic

5 Steps
of OOHA

  • Name & collar - your streety

  • Water - ensure that there is water 24x7

  • Feed - your streety twice a day

  • Time - Spend ten minutes a day with your streety

  • Medical - supplies for your streety
